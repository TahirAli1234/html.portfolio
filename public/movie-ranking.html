The Best Movies According to Tahir

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Legend of Maula Jatt

A reboot of the cult classic, it follows a fierce rivalry between a heroic fighter and a brutal gang leader.

Punjab Nahi Jaungi

A romantic drama exploring love, class differences, and cultural expectations in rural Punjab.

Bin Roye

A tale of unrequited love and emotional struggle set against a backdrop of family ties.
